Apple pie porridge

Ingredients

  • 50g porridge oats
  • 200ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 1 medium dessert apple, diced
  • 20g walnuts, sunflower seeds or pecans
  • 1 pinch of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Throw all the ingredients into a saucepan.
  2. Heat and stir until boiling, then lower the heat and simmer gently for 5 minutes, stirring often.
  3. Spoon the porridge into a serving bowl and add a sprinkle of cinnamon.
